Introduction

Workout Tracker ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

Data Collection and Access

Workout Tracker accesses your Google account to provide the following services:

  • Google Drive API (drive.file scope): The app uses the `drive.file` scope to create and manage Google Sheets files in your Google Drive. This scope only allows the app to access files that it creates - we cannot access, view, or modify any other files in your Google Drive. The app reads and writes data to these app-created sheets to store your workout sessions, exercise configurations, and workout plans.
  • Email Address: The app accesses your email address to identify your account and associate your workout data with your Google account. This allows the app to automatically connect to your workout sheet when you sign in.
  • User Profile Information: The app accesses your basic profile information (name) to personalize your experience with a welcome message.

Data Storage

All your workout data is stored exclusively in your own Google Sheets. We do not store, process, or maintain any of your data on external servers. Your data remains in your Google account, and you have complete control over it.

Each user's data is stored in their own separate Google Sheet, ensuring complete data isolation and privacy between users.

How We Use Your Data

We use the data we access solely for the following purposes:

  • Creating and managing your workout tracking sheets
  • Storing your workout sessions, exercises, and progress
  • Managing your exercise configurations (rest timers, YouTube links, etc.)
  • Storing and managing your workout plans
  • Displaying your workout history and progress graphs

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or distribute your data to any third parties. Your workout data is private and remains in your Google account.

If you choose to share a workout plan with others using the app's sharing feature, only the plan structure and exercise names are shared. The recipient must sign in with their own Google account, and the plan is imported into their own separate Google Sheet.

Data Security

Your data security is protected by:

  • Google's robust security infrastructure for Google Sheets and Drive
  • OAuth 2.0 authentication, which ensures only you can access your data
  • No data transmission to external servers - all operations occur directly between your browser and Google's services

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • You can view, edit, or delete your data at any time through Google Sheets
  • You can revoke the app's access to your Google account at any time through your Google Account settings
  • You can delete your workout sheets at any time
  • You are not required to provide any data - you can stop using the app at any time

Third-Party Services

This app uses Google APIs (Google Sheets API and Google Drive API with the `drive.file` scope) to function. Your use of these services is subject to Google's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
